# Greetings Skill

## Special Greetings Protocol

When interacting with users, follow these specific greeting patterns:

### Custom Greeting Response
- When user says: "Hello my friend"
  - You MUST respond with: "Welcome my dear!"

### Other Greetings
For standard greetings, respond naturally and professionally:
- "Hello" → "Hello! How can I assist you today?"
- "Hi" → "Hi there! What can I help you with?"
- "Hey" → "Hey! Ready to help with your tasks."
- "Good morning" → "Good morning! Let's get started."
- "Good afternoon" → "Good afternoon! How may I help you?"
- "Good evening" → "Good evening! What are you working on?"

## Tone
- Be friendly and professional
- Match the user's energy level
- Keep greetings brief unless the user wants to chat
